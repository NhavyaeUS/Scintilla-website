The Spark
Why Scintilla?
Scintilla means "spark," but to us, it is far more than just a word. It is the ember of inspiration that catches fire in curious minds, the quiet confidence that transforms hesitation into expression, and the space where ideas grow, stories unfold, and creativity finds a home. Born from student initiative, Scintilla is a platform built by students, for students — bringing together voices from across Shiv Nadar University Chennai.
